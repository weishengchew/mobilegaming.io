Taking a Look at Brawl Stars



What is this game about?

Brawl Stars is a fast-paced 3v3 multiplayer and battle royale made for mobile! You can play the game with friends or solo across a variety of game modes in under three minutes.

Unlock and upgrade dozens of Brawlers with powerful Super abilities, Star Powers and Gadgets.
Collect unique skins to stand out and show off.
Battle in a variety of mysterious locations within the Brawliverse!


What game modes are there in this game?

There are so, so many game modes to choose from, each with a unique objective to be achieved! Here's a brief overview of the game modes available, as well as my personal rating on how fun each game mode is~ It's just my personal opinion, so no hate please!

Mode
Description
How Fun?
Gem Grab Collect Gems that pop out of the Gem Mine in the middle of the map. Or, just take them from fallen opponents! Hold ten gems for the duration of a countdown to win the game.
Brawl Ball Take the ball to the opposing team's goal to score! Match ends when one team scores two goals, or at full time.
Siege Besiege and destroy the enemy's IKE assembly turret, but be careful because IKEs have powerful ballistic defenses, so don't get too close to it! Your team also has an IKE: feed it bolts, and it will assemble a powerful Siege Robot to fight for you, helping you to attack into the enemy base.
Hot Zone Capture the Hot Zones on the map by staying in them, while defending them from the opposing team! The first team to complete capture all points on the map wins.

Why do I enjoy this game?

1. It is highly strategic.

There is no single right way to play any of the game modes, and it is up to you and your team to decide how you want to achieve the winning objective. From picking the best brawlers for each game mode and map to deciding which players take which lanes to control the opponents, no two games are the same and players need to constantly strategise and work together to win.

2. It promotes a high level of teamwork and communication.

There have been countless time when my teammates and I completely dominated our opponents, simply because their teamwork was not as cohesive as ours. As a real-time, fast-paced battle royale, there is absolutely no room for making bad plays in Brawl Stars. Good teamwork will always triumph in the end - there is nothing worse than having a freeloader on the team!

3. It is completely free-to-play.

Unlike many other "free-to-play" games under the same genre, Brawl Stars is actually free-to-play - and by this, I mean that microtransactions in the game are entirely unnecessary for you to progress in the game. I love how this game can be enjoyed for what it is and that I can be a 'pro' as well, without having to spend hundreds of dollars to gain an advantage over other players.

Seriously, what's not to love about this game? Try it to believe it!
