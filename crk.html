Taking a Look at Cookie Run Kingdom



What is this game about?

Cookie Run Kingdom is a relatively new game that was released in Winter 2020 worldwide, with its official English release on January 21,2021. As a sequel to the highly-popular Cookie Run Ovenbreak, Cookie Run Kingdom is a free-to-play RPG and city builder hybrid featuring over 200 levels.

What do our friends over at PocketGamer.com have to say about this game? Let's find out:

Cookie Run: Kingdom is a game full of cutscenes and adorable graphics. As you progress, you will be shown loads of animations, bits of story, and backstory to the kingdom and the characters that you meet and hang out with...

...There are two main gameplay loops in Cookie Run: Kingdom - building up your kingdom and fighting in battles...

...You’ll need to set out your Squad, making sure they are upgraded to where they need to be, have different equipment, and are in good order before a battle...

... Take the time to level them up and make sure they have everything they need to continue to fight forward. With all of the bosses and battles looming, it’s always good to make sure everyone is in good shape to continue!

What else should I know before getting into the game?

... Alright, I'll reveal the truth. Cookie Run Kingdom is a Gacha game, which means that it implements the gacha (toy vending machine) mechanic. Similar to loot boxes (prize crate) in other video games, gacha games induce players to spend in-game currency to receive a random virtual item. While Cookie Run Kingdom is a free-to-play game, the gacha serves as an incentive to spend real-world money.

What this means is that if you intend to collect the entire roster of adorable Cookies, be prepare to spend ALOT of money...
